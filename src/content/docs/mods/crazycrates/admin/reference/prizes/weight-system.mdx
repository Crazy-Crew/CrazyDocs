<Aside type="tip">
    The system in the simplest form is that the lower the `Weight` option, the less likely chance to win the prize, or roll the tier.

    It's similar to having a pool with 500 tickets in it labeled "Chicken Suit" while 1 ticket is labeled "Hot Dog Suit"

    The likelihood of you pulling the Chicken Suit is much more common.
</Aside>

<Tabs>
    <TabCard label="What is the max range?">
        The max range is the sum of each weight option under the `Prizes` section, or `Tiers` section.

        ```yml
        Crate:
          Tiers:
            Basic:
              Weight: 20.0
          Prizes:
            "1":
               Weight: 10.0
        ```
    </TabCard>
    <TabCard label="How do I display the chance to win?">
        %chance% which tries it's best to return an accurate percentage, however with decimals. Math can only do so much.

        In the math world, at some point you will reach 0 which will display 0%. It does not mean that your prize cannot be won, the displayed chance is cosmetic.
    </TabCard>
</Tabs>
