### 1. How do I add items?
<Steps>

    1. Open up your `tiers` folder.

    2. Open up one of your `.yml` files.

    3. Scroll down to the `prize` section and copy and paste what's already there.

### 2. Why can I claim envoys but my players can't? or Why are my envoys not appearing?
<Tabs>
    <TabCard label="#1">
        Set `spawn-protection` in server.properties to 0 then restart your server!
    </TabCard>
    <TabCard label="2">
        WorldGuard made a change a few years back to how region flags involving build flags such as `block-break` or `build`.

        You no longer have to use these flags to protect your spawn, as per WorldGuard, if the person is not a member of the region. They cannot build or break by default.

        Would you like to know more? [WorldGuard's Flag Section](https://worldguard.enginehub.org/en/latest/regions/flags/)
    </TabCard>
    <TabCard label="3">
        Each tier has the ability to require a permission for a player to claim them.

        You can turn this off by going into one of your `.yml` files in the `tiers` folder.

        ```yml
        Settings:
         # If they need a permission to claim this envoy. Set too false to disable.
         Claim-Permission: false
         # What permission they need.
         Claim-Permission-Name: 'lucky.envoy.use'
        ```
    </TabCard>
</Tabs>

### 3. Why doesn't crate countdown work?
 - You need to have the option `toggle` set to `true` in your `config.yml`
  ```yml
  envoys:
    grace-period:
      # If there should be a grace period when an envoy spawns. What this means is the player will have to wait a certain amount of time.
      toggle: false
      # The time they have to wait to claim the envoy.
      timer: 120
      # The message shown above the envoy when the grace period is over.
      message: '&cReady to claim.'
      # The unit of time to show at the end of the countdown since it's in seconds. The space is required!
      time-unit: ' seconds.'
  ```
 - You also need to have [PlaceholderAPI](https://modrinth.com/plugin/placeholderapi) installed!

### 4. Can I make supply drops spawn in multiple worlds?
 - You can only make supply drops spawn in multiple worlds by using `/envoy edit`
